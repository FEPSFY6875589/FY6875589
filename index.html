Abstract

Perovskite Solar Cells (PSCs) are a new type of solar technology that is ready to be built in newer solar panels due to its high efficiency benefits, turning more sunlight into electricity than traditional silicon panels. PSCs also have a different type of material making them much more flexible compared to silicon ones. This article looks at how PSCs work, their advantages and challenges. It also explores the interesting growth of solar panels in China, including huge projects on mountains like in Guizhou and Qinghai. It will also look in how PSCs could be incorporated into these solar panels inorder to accelerate or reduce costs long term.
